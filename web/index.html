Keep the World Sweet
Founding Story! Our founder, Alec Aris, was thinking about potential business ideas for the upcoming Market Expo Day. With a passion for food and baking, Alec had the idea of creating an international cookie business, selling different types of cookies from around the world. However, after pitching the idea to friends and potential stakeholders, the business shifted from just cookies to desserts in general. Thus, the idea of Sweetly Global was born, and by combining our skills-- Alec's sociability, Olivia's technical skills, Vanessa's managerial experience, and Chloe's marketing expertise, we have succeeded in providing terrific customer service and tasty treats to our classmates and faculty.
Business and Customer Unity Sweetly Global prioritizes accuracy in our products in order to represent your cultures respectfully. We do this by taking first-hand accounts from friends and family who have a strong connection with their culture. This authenticity is the foundation of our exceptional customer satisfaction when providing our services. We enforce this by providing customer service and product feedback forms in order to gain constructive criticism and retain cultural accuracy.
Our Mission
At Sweetly Global, our mission is to celebrate cultural diversity and encourage unity by sharing traditional sweet treats from around the world, promoting cultural awareness and understanding through a universal love for food.
Our Vision
Our goal is to create a world where people from all backgrounds come together to celebrate and understand diverse cultures through the joy of sharing sweet treats. We envision a space where cultural connections thrive, leading to a more unified and respectful global community.
